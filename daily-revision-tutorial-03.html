Question 6 [Chem II - Buffer pH Change]

Buffer has pH 4.74. After adding 0.005 mol HCl to 1L buffer, pH = 4.70. Explain.

Show solution

Buffer resists pH change - small change (0.04 units) demonstrates buffer action.

If same HCl added to water, pH would drop from 7 to ~1.7!
