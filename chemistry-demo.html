Question 2 Part (a) [4 marks]

$C(s) + CO_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2CO(g)$ at 700¬∞C

$[CO] = \frac{0.10}{2.5} = 0.04$ M, $[CO_2] = \frac{0.25}{2.5} = 0.10$ M

Question 3 Part (a) [6 marks]

$S_2Cl_4(g) \rightleftharpoons 2SCl_2(g)$

3.0 moles S‚ÇÇCl‚ÇÑ in 1.5 L. At equilibrium, 0.5 moles remain. Find $K_c$.

Show solution

ICE Table:

S‚ÇÇCl‚ÇÑ dissociated: $3.0 - 0.5 = 2.5$ mol

SCl‚ÇÇ formed: $2 \times 2.5 = 5.0$ mol

$[S_2Cl_4] = \frac{0.5}{1.5} = 0.333$ M, $[SCl_2] = \frac{5.0}{1.5} = 3.333$ M

$$K_c = \frac{[SCl_2]^2}{[S_2Cl_4]} = \frac{(3.333)^2}{0.333} = \boxed{33.3}$$
