Question 1 [6 marks]

Question 4 [6 marks]

Apply Kirchhoff's rules to the circuit. Write equations for: - (a) Junction rule at B - (b) Loop 1 (FABEF) - (c) Loop 2 (BCDEB)

Need a hint?

Junction: sum of currents in = sum out. Loop: sum of potential changes = 0.

Show solution (a) Junction B: $I_1 + I_2 = I_3$ (b) Loop 1 (FABEF): $15 - 6I_1 - 3I_3 - 12 = 0$ ‚áí $2I_1 + I_3 = 1$ (c) Loop 2 (BCDEB): $-9I_2 - 12 + 3I_3 = 0$ ‚áí $I_3 - 3I_2 = 4$

‚ö†Ô∏è Nuance: Watch sign conventions: IR drop in direction of current, voltage rise across battery from - to +.
